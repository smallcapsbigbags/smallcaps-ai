The Problem With AIM News Flow

AIM generates thousands of regulatory announcements every year.

Most are administrative. Some materially alter valuation.

In the raw feed, they appear indistinguishable.

Investors are forced to manually interpret:

  • Capital raises
  • Trading updates
  • Guidance revisions
  • Contract wins
  • Balance sheet changes
  • Board movements

By the time impact is clear, the market has already moved.

How smallcaps.ai Structures AIM

Every AIM RNS passes through an impact-weighted, materiality-driven evaluation layer.

  • Categorised
  • Impact scored (1–5)
  • Summarised
  • Contextualised
  • Prioritised in the feed

The result is a feed ordered by materiality — not chronology.
